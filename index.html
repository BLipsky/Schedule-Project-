The ScheduleFinder website helps users quickly access their daily school schedule by selecting a specific day. The site has a simple layout, featuring a title that asks, "Where Should I Be Right Now?" and a dropdown menu with day options from A to G. After selecting a day and clicking "Show Schedule," users see a table showing their class periods, times, teachers, and rooms. This information is updated from a server to keep the schedule accurate.
